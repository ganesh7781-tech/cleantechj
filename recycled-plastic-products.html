Speed Breaking Plastic Rumbler

Plastic Blocks

Plastic blocks are manufactured by firmly stuffing post-consumer plastics such as chip, rubbish, and shopping bags, food wrappers, straws and stirrers, and other "single use" plastics into a plastic water bottle.

Why Sustainable Products?

Removing Waste From Landfills

One of the most common types of municipal solid waste is plastic waste. We make it a point to use waste as a resource, resulting in less plastic being wasted in landfills.

Transforming Waste Into Value

We maximize our use of plastic waste as an input source into the manufacturing of recycled plastic pallets and granules by recycling all types of plastics.

Green Future For Next Generation

In comparison to items made of virgin plastic, we recycle plastic trash, which allows us to use less energy and fossil fuels.

The Requirement of the Hour

By encouraging and increasing the rate of recycling, we are closing the loop and ensuring that plastics are kept in the product life cycle as long as possible.

All our recycled products meet quality standards comparable to virgin material — at a fraction of the environmental cost.
