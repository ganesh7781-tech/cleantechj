About Us

Home About Us

Revolutionizing Industrial Sustainability

CLEAN BIOTECH is a Palghar district-based company that recycles various forms of plastic waste and is one of the country's leading polymer waste recyclers. Our unwavering commitment to developing and maintaining a safe and clean environment has been passed down through three generations, making us a trusted partner for industries across India.

MPCB Authorized — We are fully authorized by the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board to manage industrial waste and recycling operations, ensuring 100% legal compliance for our partners.

Our Infrastructure & Expertise

Our approach includes extensive research and development. We conducted deep analysis to ensure that the quality of the plastic does not deteriorate during processing. The chain of polymer is maintained with the utmost care, resulting in recycled materials that rival virgin products in quality and durability.

Advanced Facility

Equipped with modern machinery for high-precision polymer reprocessing.

R&D Driven

Continuous investment in research to improve recycling efficiency and material quality.

Make In India

CLEAN BIOTECH takes pride in being an Indian business committed to the Government of India's Make in India initiative. We are dedicated to turning India into a worldwide manufacturing powerhouse while preserving its natural resources for the next generation.
