About Us

Leading polymer waste recyclers dedicated to a sustainable environment.

CLEAN BIOTECH is a Palghar district based company that recycles various forms of plastic waste and is one of the country’s leading polymer waste recyclers. Our unwavering commitment to developing and maintaining a safe and clean environment has been passed down through three generations.

We are the leading manufacturer of recycled plastic granules of all varieties. All sorts of plastic waste obtained from resin producers, molders, extruders, fabricators, and recycling organizations are reprocessed by us. The quality of reprocessed plastic granules made from plastic waste is comparable to that of virgin material, and they are more cost-effective than virgin material.

We specialize in producing high quality recycled plastic granules in any color to meet the needs and specifications of our customers.

What is Plastic Recycling?

Plastic recycling is the process of recovering material from plastic scraps or garbage for use in manufacturing. Plastic recycling commonly involves melting down polyester soft drink bottles and spinning the melted polymer into fibers. Plastic scraps can be recycled in a basic manner. The bales of plastic are dismantled and pulverized into little flakes. These flakes go through a procedure where the containments are separated from the actual plastic flakes, depending on the type of plastic material being recycled.
