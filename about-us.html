About Clean Biotech

About Clean Biotech

CLEAN BIOTECH is a Palghar district based company that recycles various forms of plastic waste and is one of the country's leading polymer waste recyclers. Our unwavering commitment to developing and maintaining a safe and clean environment has been passed down through three generations.

We are the leading manufacturer of recycled plastic granules of all varieties. All sorts of plastic waste obtained from resin producers, molders, extruders, fabricators, and recycling organizations are reprocessed by us. The quality of reprocessed plastic granules made from plastic waste is comparable to that of virgin material, and they are more cost-effective than virgin material.

We specialize in producing high quality recycled plastic granules in any color to meet the needs and specifications of our customers.

MPCB Authorized — We are fully authorized by the Maharashtra Pollution Control Board to manage industrial waste and recycling operations.

Infrastructure & Research

Our development approach includes extensive research and development. We conducted extensive research to ensure that the quality of the plastic does not deteriorate during processing. The chain of polymer is maintained with the utmost care.

We invest in research on a regular basis to improve our products and pass the savings on to our consumers in a variety of ways. We assure that every stage of the process is done with precision and perfection because we have over a hundred employees working in our facility.

Our Products

The Recycled Plastic Granules are made with the most advanced technologies available. What is Plastic Recycling?

Plastic recycling is the process of recovering material from plastic scraps or garbage for use in manufacturing. The bales of plastic are dismantled and pulverized into little flakes. These flakes go through a procedure where the containments are separated from the actual plastic flakes, depending on the type of plastic material being recycled.
