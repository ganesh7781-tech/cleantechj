Honored as a Pioneer in Plastic Waste Management

For almost 10 years, we've been at the forefront of plastic waste recycling. CLEAN BIOTECH Industries, founded in 2020, is a Palghar district-based company that recycles various sorts of plastic waste and is one of India's leading polymer waste recyclers.

Transparency and Traceability

From the time of collection till disposal, we provide thorough monthly reports on all papers. The best policy, in our opinion, is to work with transparency.

  • To avoid duplication, all documents are provided.
  • At the centre, third-party audits are performed to ensure that plastic waste is collected reliably.
  • Third-party audits are conducted at recycling facilities to ensure that proper recycling procedures are followed.
  • Details about transportation have been supplied.

MPCB Authorized — All our waste collection and processing operations are fully compliant with Maharashtra Pollution Control Board regulations.
