Waste Management Services

End-to-end industrial waste processing for a cleaner tomorrow.

Honored as a Pioneer in Plastic Waste Management

For almost 10 years, we’ve been at the forefront of plastic waste recycling. CLEAN BIOTECH Industries, founded in 2020, is a Palghar district-based company that recycles various sorts of plastic waste and is one of India’s leading polymer waste recyclers.

CLEAN BIOTECH has been internationally recognised as a pioneer in the field of plastic waste management over the past 10 years, having begun by making various forms of reprocessed plastic granules from waste or scrap.

Over 100 Waste Management Training Programs

Over 100 training and awareness events, as well as beach cleanup campaigns, have been held across India on waste management challenges.

We’ve also held social awareness programmes on plastic trash management at non-governmental organisations, businesses, schools, hospitals, societies, marine academies, and government agencies. We take great effort in teaching rag-pickers and collection centre employees to properly classify plastics so that they can be recycled.

Wide Network Throughout India

CLEAN BIOTECH Industries has a partnership with Municipal Corporations in all states where large amounts of plastic trash are created. We have a pan-India presence, with a network of more than 40 collection Centers maintained by a number of collection partners around the country. The majority of our partners have over 20 years of industry expertise, as well as well-established relationships and a great approach in the surrounding areas.

We collect both recyclable and non-recyclable plastic garbage every day, which is then processed at our many re-processing facilities across India. Non-recyclable waste is also transferred to environmentally sound end-of-life disposal facilities such as cement kilns or waste-to-energy plants.

MLP and Non-MLP Collection Waste is gathered by waste collectors, aggregators, traders, and societies in these locations, and our collection managers work on the ground with rag pickers to secure the best returns on the waste they collect. Every rag picker goes through a thorough training program in which they learn how to get the most out of trash and how to properly segregate rubbish according to their type.

Transparency and Traceability

From the time of collection till disposal, we provide thorough monthly reports on all papers. The best policy, in our opinion, is to work with transparency.

  • To avoid duplication, all documents are provided.
  • At the centre, third-party audits are performed to ensure that plastic waste is collected reliably.
  • Third-party audits are conducted at recycling facilities to ensure that proper recycling procedures are followed.
  • Details about transportation have been supplied.

Invoices from the Collection Center to the Recycling Plant, Weight Receipt/Copy of Transport, Inward Details at the Recycling Plant, Payment Receipt To Centers, Certificate Of Processing, Process Flow Diagram.

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

The ‘Swachh Bharat Abhiyan’ is a government of India campaign to ensure absolute cleanliness across the country. We’ve developed end-to-end solutions for waste plastic that is being wasted in large quantities every day throughout our years in the industry. We are proud to be one of the companies actively involved in the ‘Swachh Bharat Abhiyan’ an initiative by the Government of India to ensure total cleanliness across the country, with a proper training module in place in respect to collection, segregation, bailing, weighing, recycling, and processing of such waste from streets.

Providing exceptional service to our customers, eliminating pollution, and guiding industries towards a sustainable future.
